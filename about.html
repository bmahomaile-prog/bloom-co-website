About Bloom & Co

Bloom & Co is a flower shop founded in 2025 in Maseru. We started with a simple idea: to bring joy and beauty into people’s lives through fresh flowers.

Our Mission

We believe flowers are more than just gifts, they are expressions of love, joy, and care. Our mission is to provide high-quality, affordable floral products that brighten homes and hearts.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted flower shop in Lesotho, known for quality and creativity.
