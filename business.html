Our Business Model

Bloom & Co generates revenue by selling flowers directly in our shop, through online orders, and by offering delivery services for events and special occasions.

How It Works

  1. Customers place orders online or visit our shop.
  2. We prepare fresh flowers daily to ensure quality.
  3. Orders are delivered to homes, offices, or events.
  4. We also provide special packages for weddings and corporate functions.

Our goal is to combine traditional flower sales with modern online convenience, making it easy for customers to enjoy fresh blooms anytime.
